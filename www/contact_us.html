Contact Us


If you have any questions, suggestions, or would like to make contributions to the SJP Data Catalog, feel free to contact Hannah De los Santos at hdelossantos@mitre.org or the Social Justice Platform at socialjustice@mitre.org.

Please make sure to include "SJP Data Catalog" in the subject line of your email to indicate that your inquiry is related to this data catalog.

If you are reaching out to suggest new resources to be included in the catalog, please document the data sources using the format below:
