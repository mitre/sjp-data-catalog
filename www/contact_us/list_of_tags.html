List of Tags and Defintions

Resource Types:

Data Methodology Methodology/procedure for computing a novel measure using a set of datasources
Data Repository Collection of multiple datasets
Dataset Singular source of data
Summary Table Tables including summary statistics and other aggregated values from a dataset
Tool Interactive tool (e.g. dashboard, atlas, map)

Data Attributes:

Adult Relating to adults (i.e. ages 18 or older)
Youth Relating to youth/minors (i.e. ages under 18)
Age Stratified Examines difference and possible inequalities among different ages
Disability Stratified Examines difference and possible inequalities among those with/without disabilities
Education Stratified Examines difference and possible inequalities among different education levels
Ethnicity Stratified Examines difference and possible inequalities among different ethnicities
Gender Stratified Examines difference and possible inequalities among different genders
Income Stratified Examines difference and possible inequalities among different income levels
Race Stratified Examines difference and possible inequalities among different races
SOGI Stratified Examines difference and possible inequalities among different sexual orientations and gender identities
Individual Each datum represents a single individual
Population Each datum represents a population or group of individuals
Survey Data drawn from a survey administered to a group of individuals
Cross-sectional A set of observations at a single point in time
Time Series A fixed set of observations at multiple points in time
Longitudinal A set of observations drawn from the same population at multiple points in time
Urban/Rural Stratified Examines difference and possible inequalities among different types of geographical locations

Data Subjects:

Accessibility Contains information related to the accessibility that people have to various places, resources, etc.
Built Environment Contains information related to the physical parts of a place (e.g., homes, buildings, streets, open spaces, and infrastructure)
Crime Contains information related to the occurrence of crime and misconduct
Cultural Assets Contains information related to the cultural make-up of a community
Demographics Includes demographic categories and markers
Discrimination Contains information related to discrimination of individuals/groups based on certain characteristics
Economy Contains information related to the economic condition of people or places
Education Contains information related to education, schools, students, etc.
Environment Contains information related to the state of the physical environment in a particular place
Government Contains information related to features of government or government-related activities
Health Contains information dealing generally with health-related phenomena
Health Status Contains information related to the health status of individuals
Healthcare Access Contains information related to individuals' access to healthcare
Healthcare Status Contains information related to the status or possession of an individual's healthcare
Household Composition and Disability Contains information on the family composition of households and their disability status
Housing Contains information related to housing situations and conditions
Justice Contains information related to the criminal justice system
Minority Status and Language Contains information related to an individual's status as a minority and the non-English languages they may speak
Mortality Contains information related to the deaths of individuals
Physical Infrastructure Contains information related to the basic physical structures required for an economy to function (e.g. transportation, power grid, sewerage and waste disposal systems)
Police Contains information related to police activity
Racial Equity Examining equity across different races
Social Capital Contains information related to set of shared values that allows individuals to work together in a group to effectively achieve a common purpose
Social Context Contains information related to the settings in which people in a group/commnity interact with each other
Social Determinants of Health Pertaining to the conditions in of a person's environment that affects health, functioning, and quality-of-life outcomes and risks
Socioeconomic Status Contains information related to the social standing or class of an individual or group
Structural Racism Contains information related to structural and institutionalized racism
Technology Contains information related to technological accessibility and usage
Transportation Contains information related to the state, accessibility, or methods of transporation in an area
Wellbeing Contains information related to the overall wellbeing (phyiscal, mental) of individuals
